Regentra billing is managed through Stripe and covers your subscription plan, PSA seat count, and compliance tenant allocation.

## Trial period

Every new Regentra account starts with a **14-day free trial** that includes full access to all features — compliance, PSA, integrations, and the customer portal. No credit card is required to start the trial.

<Note>
  When the trial expires, you must select a subscription plan to continue using Regentra. Your data is preserved for 30 days after trial expiration.
</Note>

## Subscription plans

Regentra offers tiered subscription plans based on your MSP's size and needs. Visit [regentra.io/pricing](https://regentra.io/pricing) for current plan details and pricing.

All plans include:
- Compliance framework management
- PSA ticketing and service desk
- Customer portal
- Integrations (Entra ID, Level.io, Stripe)
- AI-powered ticket drafts

Plans differ in:
- Number of included PSA seats
- Number of compliance tenants
- Support tier and response times

## Per-seat billing (PSA)

PSA seats are billed per user per month. Each team member who needs access to the PSA module (creating tickets, logging time, managing the service desk) requires a seat. Users with compliance-only access do not consume PSA seats.

## Compliance tenant billing

Each managed client that uses compliance features occupies one compliance tenant. Your plan includes a base number of tenants. Additional tenants can be purchased as add-ons.

## Stripe integration

Regentra uses Stripe for all platform billing:

- Subscription charges are processed automatically each billing cycle
- Payment methods (credit card, ACH) are managed through the Stripe customer portal
- Invoices and receipts are available in **Settings → Billing**

<Info>
  This is separate from the per-org Stripe integration used for PSA client invoicing. Platform billing covers your Regentra subscription. The [PSA Stripe integration](/integrations/stripe) handles invoicing your clients.
</Info>

## Managing your subscription

Go to **Settings → Billing** to:

- View your current plan and usage
- Add or remove PSA seats
- Purchase additional compliance tenants
- Update payment method
- View invoice history
- Cancel or change your plan

<Warning>
  Downgrading your plan may reduce your seat or tenant limits. Ensure your current usage fits within the new plan's limits before downgrading.
</Warning>
