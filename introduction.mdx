Regentra is a unified platform that brings **compliance management** and **professional services automation (PSA)** together in one place. Built specifically for MSPs, Regentra lets you manage multi-framework compliance programs and day-to-day service operations for every client tenant from a single dashboard at [app.regentra.io](https://app.regentra.io).

## Two Core Modules

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Compliance">
    Adopt and manage multiple compliance frameworks simultaneously. Regentra's Common Control Framework (CCF) maps your internal controls across frameworks so you implement once and satisfy many.

    **Supported frameworks:** HIPAA 2026, SOC 2, NIST CSF, ISO 27001, CMMC Level 1, CMMC Level 2, PCI DSS, GDPR, and FTC Safeguards.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PSA">
    Run your service desk, track time, manage SLAs, invoice clients, and maintain a knowledge base — all integrated with your compliance workflows.

    **Capabilities:** Ticketing, SLA management, time tracking, invoicing, knowledge base, and asset management.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Who Is Regentra For?

Regentra is purpose-built for **Managed Service Providers** who handle compliance and IT operations for clients in regulated industries:

- **Healthcare** — HIPAA compliance programs, security risk assessments, BAA tracking
- **Financial services** — SOC 2 readiness, PCI-DSS controls, FTC Safeguards implementation
- **Government contractors** — CMMC certification prep, NIST CSF alignment
- **Any organization** handling sensitive data across GDPR, ISO 27001, or multiple overlapping frameworks

If you manage compliance for more than one client — or across more than one framework — Regentra eliminates the spreadsheet sprawl and manual tracking that slows you down.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register at [app.regentra.io](https://app.regentra.io) with your organization name, industry, and admin details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your modules">
    The onboarding wizard walks you through activating Compliance, PSA, or both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adopt a framework">
    Head to the Frameworks page and click **Adopt** on your first compliance framework. Controls are seeded automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **Settings → Team → Invite** to bring in compliance officers, technicians, and client stakeholders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect integrations">
    Link Microsoft Entra ID, Level.io, or cloud providers to start pulling automated evidence.
  </Step>
</Steps>
