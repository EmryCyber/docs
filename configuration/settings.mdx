The Regentra dashboard gives you full control over your account, team, and workspace configuration. This page covers the settings available and how to update them.

## Account settings

Your account settings control your personal profile and login credentials. To access them, go to **Settings → Account**.

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Profile name** | The name displayed to teammates and in notifications |
| **Email** | The address used for login and system emails |
| **Password** | Update your password at any time |
| **Timezone** | Controls timestamps in the dashboard and notification delivery times |

## Workspace settings

Workspace settings apply to your entire organization. Only **Owners** and **Admins** can modify these.

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Workspace name** | The display name for your organization across the platform |
| **Logo / branding** | Upload a custom logo shown in the dashboard and exported reports |
| **Default language** | Sets the language for dashboard UI and system-generated messages |
| **Date format** | Choose how dates appear (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY) |

## Team management

### Inviting team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Go to **Settings → Team** in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send an invitation">
    Click **Invite**, enter the team member's email address, and select their role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the invitation">
    The invitee receives an email with a link to accept. They appear as **Pending** until they accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Roles and permissions

Each team member is assigned one of three roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access: manage billing, delete the workspace, assign all roles, and configure all settings |
| **Admin** | Manage team members, configure workspace settings, create and edit workflows and resources |
| **Member** | View and run workflows, access resources they've been granted, and manage their own profile |

### Removing members

To remove a team member, go to **Settings → Team**, locate the member, and click **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately. Any workflows or resources they owned are reassigned to the workspace Owner.

## Billing settings

Go to **Settings → Billing** to manage your subscription.

- **Usage**: View your current usage against plan limits for API calls, workflows, and active resources.
- **Upgrade**: Click **Upgrade plan** to compare tiers and select a new plan.
- **Payment methods**: Add, update, or remove credit cards and view past invoices.

<Note>
  Some settings changes — such as modifying the workspace name, date format, or default language — take effect immediately and are visible to all team members.
</Note>
