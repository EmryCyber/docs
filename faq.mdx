Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Regentra. If your question isn't covered here, reach out to our support team at [support@regentra.io](mailto:support@regentra.io).

## Account & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. Regentra offers a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card is required to start — just sign up and begin exploring the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What plans are available?">
    Regentra offers three plans: **Starter**, **Growth**, and **Enterprise**. Each plan includes a different set of workflow limits, team seats, and support options. Visit the [pricing page](https://regentra.io/pricing) for a full breakdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Go to **Settings → Billing → Change Plan** and select the plan you want. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes. You can export resources and workflow run history as CSV or JSON directly from the dashboard. Go to the relevant section and look for the **Export** option in the action menu.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## API & Integrations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there an API rate limit?">
    Yes. The default rate limit is **1,000 requests per minute** per API key. If you exceed the limit, you'll receive a `429 Too Many Requests` response. Enterprise customers can request higher limits by contacting support.

    <Tip>
      Implement exponential backoff in your client to handle rate limit responses gracefully. See the [troubleshooting guide](/troubleshooting) for an example.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which integrations are supported?">
    Regentra integrates with **Slack**, **GitHub**, **Jira**, and email out of the box. You can also connect to any external service using webhooks for custom integrations. See the [integrations guide](/guides/integrations) for setup instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you have a sandbox or test environment?">
    Yes. Create a **Staging** environment in **Settings → Environments** and use the API keys from that environment for testing. Data in staging environments is isolated from production.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?">
    All data is stored in SOC 2 compliant infrastructure located in the United States. EU data residency is available on Enterprise plans — contact support to enable it for your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Regentra SOC 2 certified?">
    Yes. Regentra maintains SOC 2 Type II certification. If you need a copy of the report for your security review, contact [support@regentra.io](mailto:support@regentra.io).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a security vulnerability?">
    Email [security@regentra.io](mailto:security@regentra.io) with a description of the vulnerability, steps to reproduce, and any relevant details. We aim to respond within 24 hours and will keep you updated on our progress.

    <Note>
      Please do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly until we've had a chance to investigate and issue a fix.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Workflows & Automation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many workflows can I create?">
    Workflow limits depend on your plan:

    | Plan       | Workflow limit |
    | ---------- | -------------- |
    | Starter    | 10             |
    | Growth     | 100            |
    | Enterprise | Unlimited      |

    To increase your limit, upgrade your plan in **Settings → Billing**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can workflows run on a schedule?">
    Yes. Regentra supports cron-based scheduling for workflows. You can set a cron expression directly in the workflow trigger configuration. See the [workflows guide](/guides/workflows) for details and examples.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a workflow fails?">
    Failed runs are logged with full error details, including which action failed and why. You can view this in **Workflows → [workflow name] → Run History**. You can also configure notifications to alert your team when a workflow fails — see the [notifications guide](/guides/notifications).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
